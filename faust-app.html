faust.app

Quick creation of a stylish invoice for entrepreneurs

Faust is a web app that helps entrepreneurs create stylish invoices quickly and easily. The app takes care of all the necessary calculations, including health and social insurance and income tax, so users can focus on their business.

Challenges

  • One of the biggest challenges I faced was designing an app that was both easy to use and visually appealing. I wanted to create an app that would be intuitive for users, but I also wanted it to have a modern and stylish look.
  • Another challenge was ensuring that the app was accurate and compliant with all relevant regulations. This involved a lot of research and testing to make sure that the app was calculating everything correctly.

solutions

  • I solved the challenges I faced by using a user-centered design approach. I started by conducting user research to understand the needs of my target audience. I then created wireframes and prototypes to test different design ideas. Once I had a design that I was happy with, I conducted usability testing to make sure that the app was easy to use.
  • I also worked closely with the developers to ensure that the app was compliant with all relevant regulations. This involved a lot of communication and collaboration to make sure that we were all on the same page.

results

  • Faust was launched in 2020 and has been well-received by users.
  • I am proud of the work I did on Faust and I believe that the app is a valuable tool for entrepreneurs. The app has helped to simplify the invoice creation process and has saved users time and money.
